Dubai

Futuristic Luxury • Culture • Safari

Overview

Dubai is known for its futuristic skyscrapers, luxury shopping, and ambition to build the world's biggest and best structures, contrasting with its historical roots on the Arabian Gulf.

Best time to Visit

  1. November - April Ideal for outdoor activities, sightseeing, and festivals
  2. May, Sep, Oct Good for beach lovers as the sea is warm
  3. June - August Very hot but the cheapest time to visit due to fewer tourists

Top Places to Visit

  1. Burj Khalifa
  2. The Dubai Mall
  3. Dubai Foundation
  4. Palm Jumeirah
  5. Desert Safari
  6. Jumeirah Mosque
  7. Dubai Frame

Best Adventure

  1. Desert Safari
  2. Waterparks - Atlantis, The Palm
  3. Indoor Skydiving - iFly Dubai
  4. Theme Parks - IMG Worlds of Adventure, Dubai Parks and Resorts

Best Nightlife

  1. Dubai Marina & JBR
  2. Downtown Dubai

Famous Festivlas

  1. Dubai Shopping Festival (DSF)
  2. Dubai Design Week & Art Dubai
  3. Dubai Jazz Festival & Dubai World Cup

Best Temples & Churches

  1. Jumeirah Mosque
  2. St. Mary's Catholic Church & All Saints Cathedral
  3. Guru Nanak Darbar Gurunanak

Travel Tips

  1. Dress modestly, especially in religious places (cover shoulders and knees)
  2. Mainly use Dubai Metro for travel which covers key areas
  3. Drink plenty of water, particularly in warmer months
  4. Check visa requirements for your nationality
  5. Be mindful of local customs, particularly during Ramadan
