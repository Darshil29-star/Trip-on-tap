Kerala

Backwaters • Nature • Ayurveda • Peace

Overview

Kerala, known as "Gods's Own Country" , is famous for its serene backwaters, lush greenery, hill stations, beaches and rich cultural heritage.

Best Time to Visit

September to March is the best time to visit Kerala due to pleasent weather.

Top Places to Visit

  1. Alleppey Backwaters
  2. Munnar Hill Station
  3. Wayanad
  4. Kovalam Beach

Best Nightlife at

  1. Kochi & Trivandrum
  2. Kovalam & Varkala
  3. Houseboats

Famous Festivals & Culture

  1. Onam
  2. Vishu
  3. Thrissur Pooram
  4. Kathakali (Dance)

Famous Temples and Churches

  1. Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple (Trivandrum)
  2. Guruvayur Temple, Vadakkunnathan Temple (Thrissur)
  3. St. Francis Church (Kochi - India's oldest European church)
  4. Mattancherry Palace (Synagogue)

Travel Tips

  1. Consider renting a car with a driver for hills
  2. Try local seafood, appam, puttu, and fresh coconut dishes
  3. Experience authentic Ayurvedic treatments
  4. Explore spice markets in Thekkady and Kochi
  5. Dress modestly in temples (cover shoulders/knees)
