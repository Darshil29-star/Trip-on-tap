Manali & Kasol

Adventure • Snow-capped peaks • Backpacker • Bustling hub

Overview

Manali ha sonwy winters and pleasant summers, great for adventure. Kasol, nestled in Parvati Valley, is a backpacker's paradise known for its tranquility.

Best time to Visit

  1. March-June March-June
  2. September-November (clear skies)
  3. December-February (snowfall, skiing)

Top Places & Adventure

  1. Solang Valley
  2. Vashisht Hot Springs
  3. Beas River
  4. Buddhist Monastery
  5. Skiing/Paragliding (Solang)
  6. Parvati River
  7. Chalal Village
  8. River Rafting (Beas)
  9. Trekking

Famous Festivals & Culture

  1. Dussehra in Kullu Valley (near Manali)
  2. Local Himachali festivals celebrated with vibrant fairs

Famous Temples & Churches

  1. Hadimba Devi Temple
  2. Vashisht Temple
  3. Manikaran has Gurudwara and Ramchandra Temple

Travel Tips

  1. Weather changes drastically; carry warm clothes for evenings and snow
  2. Roads can be tricky in winter; consider taxis or local buses
  3. Book the hotel and resorts early for best deals
  4. Respect the local culture and people
