Goa, India

Beaches • Nightlife • Culture • Festivals

Overview

Goa is a India's most popular beach destination, famous for its golden beaches, Portugese heritage, vibrant nightlife, delicious seafood, and relaxed lifestyle. It attracts tourists from all over the world.

Best time to Visit

the best time to visit Goa is from November to February when the wheather is pleasent and festivals are in full swing.

Top Places to Visit

  1. Baga Beach
  2. Calangute Beach
  3. Anjuna Beach
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls
  5. Fort Aguada
  6. Chapora Fort

Best Cafes & Nightlife

  1. Cafe Mambo
  2. Tito's Lane
  3. Britto's Cafe
  4. Curlies Beach Shack
  5. Thalassa

Famous Festivals

  1. Sunburn Festival (EDM Music Festival)
  2. Goa Carnival
  3. Christmas & New Year Celebrations
  4. Shigmo Festival

Temples & Churches

  1. Basilica of Born Jesus
  2. Se Cathedral
  3. Mangeshi Temple
  4. Shanta Durga Temple

Travel Tips

  1. Rent Scooters or bikes for easy travel
  2. Carry sunscreen and light clothing
  3. Respect local culture and churches
  4. Book stays early during peak seasons
