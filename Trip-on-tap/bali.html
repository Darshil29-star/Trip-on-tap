Bali, Indonesia

Beaches • Cafes • Temples • landscapes

Overview

Bali is known for its unique Hindu culure, volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches, and spiritual atmosphere.

Best time to Visit

  1. April to October Sunny, low humidity, ideal for beaches, surfing, hiking; peak tourist months are July, August, and late December
  2. April, May, Sep, Oct Great weather, fewer crowds, better prices
  3. November to March Lush green landscapes, cultural ceremonies, fewer tourists, but with heavy afternoon rains

Top Places to Visit

  1. Ubud
  2. Uluwatu
  3. Seminayak/Canggu
  4. Nusa Penida/Islands
  5. Kuta

Nightlife & Cafes

Canggu/Seminyak: Home to trendy bars, beach clubs (like Finns Beach Club), and cafes that transition into party spots.

Famous Festivals

  1. Nyepi
  2. Bali Arts Festival (June to July)
  3. Independence Day(August 17)

Famous Temples

  1. Uluwatu
  2. Tanah Lot
  3. Tira Empul

Travel Tips

  1. Do early booking during the peak seasons
  2. Prefer to rent the scooter to explore, but be cautions.
  3. Respect local customs, dress modestly for temples(sarongs often provided).
